Core Functionalities

Automatic Tracking

Trips are logged automatically using phone sensors. No manual start/stop needed.

Smart Mode Detection

Intelligently detects if you are walking, cycling, in a car, bus, or metro.

Offline-First Storage

Works perfectly without an internet connection. Your trips are stored locally and sync later.

Export & Share

Export your trip history as PDF or CSV for insurance, expense claims, or personal records.

Carbon Footprint

Calculates your carbon savings for eco-friendly trips like walking and cycling.

Privacy-First

Your travel data is stored on your device by default. You control what you share.

Gamification

Earn badges and rewards for achieving milestones and choosing eco-friendly travel.

Cloud Sync

Optionally sync your data to the cloud to back up and access your history anywhere.
